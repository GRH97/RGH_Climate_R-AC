About RGH

Do the job properly. The first time.

RGH Climate was built on a simple idea: do the job properly, the first time. We install, maintain, and repair air conditioning, heating, and refrigeration systems for homes and businesses, bringing the kind of attention to detail that keeps systems running efficiently for years, not months.

Most modern air conditioning systems don't just cool — they heat too, using built-in reverse-cycle technology to keep a space comfortable in the depths of winter as well as the height of summer. We make sure every system we install and service is set up to use this heating function properly, so you're not paying for a unit that only earns its keep for half the year.

Every job is carried out by fully certified engineers, using quality parts and proper diagnostics — not guesswork. Whether it's a single split system in a home office or a commercial refrigeration unit that can't afford downtime, we treat every job with the same level of care.
